What We Do

Rapid advancements in low-cost sensor and communication technology approaches have the potential to help improving the use effectiveness and sustainability of water. iMoMo presents a new vision to address global water challenges through crowd-sourced, open-innovation monitoring.

iMoMo embraces the concept that any stakeholder (farmers, local organizations, NGOs, industry, governmental bodies, etc.) can contribute any type of data to the iMoMo knowledge system, including quantitative and qualitative data. It relies on state-of-the-art open innovation technology for achieving this and is built around a backbone architecture which has been designed with openness, extensibility and portability in mind.
